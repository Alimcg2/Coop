The Bean Basket

Come visit us at our bulk buying store, the Bean Basket, located in HUB 131K at the University of Washington!

We serve non-perishable, sustainable sourced foods at the lowest prices we can. As we are all volunteer run and do not need to pay for overhead, we are able to pass those savings on to you. Keep your eyes open as we move closer to opening our doors.

If you would like us to place any specific orders for you with our suppliers, please contact us at asuwsfc@uw.edu or call us at 206-543-8997
